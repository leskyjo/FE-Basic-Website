## üéØ PLATFORM MISSION

**Felon Entrepreneur** is a comprehensive life-rebuilding platform designed to help people transitioning from incarceration or hardship rebuild their lives across multiple dimensions: career, health, finances, resources, and education.

**Core Promise:** Turn a user's situation into specialized, actionable plans across key life areas, then provide tools, resources, and community to execute those plans.

## üèóÔ∏è PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE

### Dual-Path Foundation

**Professional Path:** Employee-focused (career advancement, job search, promotions)  
**Entrepreneur Path:** Business-focused (startup launch, scaling, empire building)

### Multi-Plan System (NEW)

Instead of one "Life Plan," users get specialized plans:

1. **Career Plan** (formerly "Life Plan")
   - Professional version: Career roadmap (junior ‚Üí senior ‚Üí manager ‚Üí executive)
   - Entrepreneur version: Business milestones (idea ‚Üí MVP ‚Üí revenue ‚Üí scaling)

2. **Health & Wellness Plan**
   - Physical health priorities
   - Mental health support
   - Substance use recovery
   - Stress management

3. **Financial Plan**
   - Income optimization
   - Debt management
   - Savings goals
   - Credit repair

4. **Resources Plan**
   - Housing assistance
   - Food/transportation help
   - Legal services connections
   - Probation/parole support

5. **Education Plan**
   - Skills development roadmap
   - Course recommendations
   - Certification paths
   - Career training

**Each plan:**
- Has its own questionnaire
- Gets AI-generated roadmap
- Has dedicated AI coaching context
- Tracks progress independently
- Uses Horizons structure (Week 1 ‚Üí Month 1 ‚Üí 90 Days ‚Üí 6 Months ‚Üí 1 Year ‚Üí 5 Years)

## üì± CORE FEATURES

### 1. Career Finder Studio (Jobs)

**What it is:** Intelligent job search and application system

**Features:**
- Job search with filters (felon-friendly, location, type, salary)
- Job cards with AI-powered matching scores
- Saved jobs (tier-limited: Starter 5, Plus 15, Pro 30)
- Resume Builder (Plus: 5/mo, Pro: 10/mo)
- Application Assistant (Starter: 1 sample, Plus: 15/mo, Pro: 30/mo)
- Interview Prep (Plus: 3/mo, Pro: 6/mo)

**Path-Specific:**
- Professional: Traditional W-2 jobs, promotions, career opportunities
- Entrepreneur: Contract work, freelance gigs, business opportunities

**Tier:** All tiers (with limits)

---

### 2. BMB (Build My Business) - ENTREPRENEUR EXCLUSIVE

**What it is:** Interactive business development coach with visual workflow

**Features:**
- User explains business idea via chat
- AI generates visual workflow (N8N-style nodes/steps)
- Each step is clickable (detailed guidance + resources + links)
- Right-click to mark steps complete (progress tracking)
- Covers: Idea validation ‚Üí Registration ‚Üí Launch ‚Üí Marketing ‚Üí Scaling
- Ongoing coaching post-launch

**Examples of steps:**
- Validate business idea
- Register business name
- Get EIN (irs.gov link)
- Open business bank account
- Build MVP/website
- First marketing campaign
- Acquire first customer
- Optimize operations
- Scale revenue

**Tier:** 
- Plus: Primary path only (if Entrepreneur chosen first)
- Pro: Full access
- ProTeams: Owner + 3 seats full access, additional seats if Entrepreneur path

---

### 3. BMC (Build My Career) - PROFESSIONAL EXCLUSIVE

**What it is:** Interactive career advancement roadmap with visual timeline

**Features:**
- AI-driven skill gap analysis recommending FE products and services for professional development
- User explains current role + career goals
- AI generates career roadmap (timeline format)
- Each milestone is clickable (skills, timeline, strategies, resources)
- Right-click to mark milestones complete
- Covers: Current role ‚Üí Promotions ‚Üí Leadership ‚Üí Executive
- Ongoing career coaching

**Examples of milestones:**
- Master current role (6 months)
- Lead a project (3 months)
- Get first direct report (12 months)
- Become senior (18 months)
- Transition to manager (24 months)
- Manage multiple teams (3 years)
- Director level (4 years)
- VP/C-suite (5+ years)

**Tier:**
- Plus: Primary path only (if Professional chosen first)
- Pro: Full access
- ProTeams: Owner + 3 seats full access, additional seats if Professional path

---

### 4. Resources Directory

**What it is:** Local, state, and federal resources hub

**Categories:**
- **Housing:** Shelters, transitional housing, permanent housing assistance
- **Food:** Food banks, SNAP, meal programs
- **Transportation:** Bus passes, vehicle assistance, ride shares
- **Drug & Alcohol:** Treatment centers, AA/NA meetings, recovery programs
- **Mental Health:** Counseling, therapy, crisis hotlines
- **Family & Children:** Childcare, parenting classes, family services
- **Legal Services:** Probation/parole offices, attorney referrals, legal aid
- **Other:** Internet access, phone services, clothing assistance

**Information Provided:**
- Organization name
- Physical address
- Mailing address
- Phone number
- Website
- Email
- Hours of operation
- Eligibility requirements

**Location-Based:**
- Detects user's ZIP code
- Shows local resources first
- Includes state and federal options
- Search/filter by category

**Tier:** All tiers (free feature)

---

### 5. Education & Courses (PRIMARY REVENUE DRIVER)

**What it is:** Training courses with certification and job placement

**Course Types:**
1. **Credit Repair Courses**
   - How to fix credit
   - Dispute process
   - Building credit from scratch
   - Credit score optimization

2. **Business Startup Courses**
   - LLC formation
   - Business planning
   - Marketing fundamentals
   - Sales strategies
   - Operations management
   - Financial management

3. **Career Skills Courses**
   - Leadership development
   - Communication skills
   - Technical skills (coding, design, etc.)
   - Industry certifications
   - Management training

**Course Features:**
- Video lessons
- Downloadable resources
- Quizzes/tests
- Certificate upon completion
- **Job placement assistance** (post-completion)

**Pricing:**
- Per-course purchase ($29-$199 depending on course)
- Pro users get discounts (10-25%)
- Course completion grants bonus tokens (1 resume, 1 app assist, 1 interview prep)

**Tier:** All tiers can purchase (viewing/browsing free, purchasing requires payment)

### 6. Community

**What it is:** Social networking for Felon Entrepreneur members

**Features:**
- User profiles
- Follow/connect with other members
- Direct messaging
- Group discussions
- Support circles
- Mentor matching (future)

**Tier:** All tiers (free feature)

---

### 7. Stories

**What it is:** User-generated content platform for sharing experiences

**Features:**
- Post stories (video, images, or text)
- Like, share, comment on stories
- Follow users whose stories resonate
- Story categories (success stories, challenges overcome, advice, etc.)
- Weekly featured story

**Weekly Featured Story:**
- Admins select best story of the week
- Winner gets invited to 30-minute podcast with founders
- Podcast recorded and published on YouTube
- Winner gets exposure + recognition

**Tier:** All tiers (free feature)

---

### 8. Donations & Sponsorship

**What it is:** Two-way donation platform

**8A. User Donations to FE:**
- One-time donations
- Monthly recurring donations
- Donor leaderboard (monthly)
- Top donor gets choice of 3 appreciation packages
- Transparency videos showing how funds are used

**8B. Sponsorship Program:**
- Users in tough situations can apply for sponsorship
- Application reviewed/vetted by FE team
- Approved users post their story (video/text/images)
- Other users can sponsor them (financial support)
- Progress updates from sponsored users
- Success stories shared publicly

**Sponsorship Application:**
1. User submits situation explanation
2. FE team reviews for legitimacy
3. Approved users post on Sponsorship board
4. Sponsors choose who to support
5. Sponsored users provide updates
6. Success tracked and shared

**Tier:** All tiers (free to apply, free to donate/sponsor)

---

### 9. AI Coach (FE Coach)

**What it is:** Contextual AI assistant available throughout app

**Contexts:**
- Career Plan coaching
- Health Plan coaching
- Financial Plan coaching
- Resources navigation help
- Course recommendations
- General life advice

**Tier-Based Access:**
- Starter: 10 AI credits/week (universal)
- Plus: Unlimited with soft caps
- Pro: Unlimited with higher caps + weekly proactive check-ins

**Path-Aware:**
- Professional path: Career advancement focus
- Entrepreneur path: Business building focus

**Tier:** All tiers (with usage limits)

---

### 10. Specialized Plans (Multi-Plan System)

**5 Plan Types:**

**1. Career Plan**
- Questionnaire: Employment status, goals, industry, timeline
- Generated: Week 1 ‚Üí Year 5 roadmap
- AI coaching: Career advancement strategies

**2. Health & Wellness Plan**
- Questionnaire: Physical health, mental health, substance use, stress
- Generated: Week 1 ‚Üí Year 5 wellness roadmap
- AI coaching: Health optimization, recovery support

**3. Financial Plan**
- Questionnaire: Income, expenses, debt, goals
- Generated: Week 1 ‚Üí Year 5 financial roadmap
- AI coaching: Budgeting, debt payoff, savings strategies

**4. Resources Plan**
- Questionnaire: Housing, food, transportation, legal needs
- Generated: Immediate resources + long-term stability roadmap
- AI coaching: Resource navigation, stability planning

**5. Education Plan**
- Questionnaire: Current skills, desired skills, learning style, time
- Generated: Week 1 ‚Üí Year 5 education roadmap
- AI coaching: Course recommendations, certification paths

**Each plan independent:**
- Can be generated separately
- Has own AI coaching context
- Tracks progress individually
- Can be regenerated (tier-limited)

**Tier:** 
- Starter: Can generate plans (limited regenerations)
- Plus: More regenerations (4/month total across all plans)
- Pro: Even more regenerations (8/month total across all plans)
